<MORE INFORMATION/>

The data

In collaboration with Bibnet and the Public library of Ghent we're giving away a ton of library data, which you can use in your own development. This is unique in Belgium.

Next to the thematic datasets all other open datasets are available to build upon your idea as well. Combining data makes for interesting applications.

In order to gain insight into the data and the challenges, we're organising a data-dive on the 9th of March 2016. During this meetup we will explain more about the data and the technical aspects so that you can start to think about your innovative idea.

The hackathon

On the 19th of March we're hacking away at the Campus Kantienberg of Arteveldehogeschool.

You can hack away in the from scratch hackathon between 09:00 en 15:00. Around 15:30 you have only a few minutes to pitch your project to a jury of experts.

After the deliberation, we announce the winners of this years hackathon and celebrate with a drink on the house.

Who can participate?

Anyone, from profesionnal coders to open advocates, citizens, students or designers can particpate. The more the merrier. You can be a team of one of a whole group of people.

Want to join a team on the day itself? Stop by and we'll see in which team we can fit you in.
